System output rate

How fast your sprinklers put down water, in inches per hour. Don't know it exactly? 0.75"/hr is a reasonable estimate for most residential systems — see the tip below for how to measure it precisely.

How do I find my real output rate?
Place 4–6 empty tuna cans (or similar straight-sided containers) around one zone. Run that zone for 15 minutes. Measure the water depth in each can with a ruler and average them. Multiply that average by 4 to get inches per hour. Example: cans average 0.25" after 15 minutes → 1.0"/hour.
